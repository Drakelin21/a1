Goals

The goals of this website is to connect more with people on the internet so that the cosplay shop receives more attention. It is also made easier for people to sign up for parties as well.

Success Evaluation

Site's success will be evaluated by the amount of sign ups and the shop's rise in popularity.

Target Audience

The target audience is mainly teenagers and young adults who are interested in dressing up as their favourite characters in a comic, anime, movies, etc. The site is designed to please and catch the eyes of the teenagers and young adults with a red theme which will not be too bright and not too dark.
